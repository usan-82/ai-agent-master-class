### OpenAI says it will discontinue AI video platform Sora  
Six months after launch, OpenAI will sunset Sora—its standalone AI video-generation tool—and fold its GAN-diffusion technology into ChatGPT and DALL·E. The change, announced in a corporate blog post, aims to streamline subscriptions and deliver a unified multimodal interface. Existing subscribers will keep access through April and receive credits toward other OpenAI services. Analysts say the consolidation should drive broader adoption by eliminating tool fragmentation, though advanced users may miss specialized editing controls.
